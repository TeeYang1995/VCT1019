Our Process

1

First Contact

When we get an order from you, we will reach out to you via phone or email and set up a meeting. We will let you know what to prepare for our upcoming meeting. 

2

Meeting

We will meet with you via video chat or over the phone. During this step, we will try our best to get to know you and your business. We can exchange more information and answer each other's questions. We will also talk and learn more about what you want in your commercial.

3

Concepts

After meeting with you, our animators will the come up with a few concepts and storyboards for your commercials. We will then contact you once more to finalize the rough animation. Once we get a green light, we will start creating the actual animation. 

4

Working Progress

We will continue updating you as we work on your animation to ensure you are satisfied with the animation. 

5

Final Step

We will show you the final product, discuss pricing, and if you are happy with it, then we will send it to the distribution companies to conclude our project with you.
